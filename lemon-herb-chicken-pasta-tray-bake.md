# Lemon & Herb Chicken Pasta Tray Bake

Solid 7/10 — would've been a 10 if the pasta was al dente. Pre-cook it first and it's there.

**Source:** Louis Howard PT (credit: @Dave Fell)  
**Tags:** Chicken, Pasta, High Protein, Tray Bake, Main  
**Equipment:** Oven, large ovenproof dish

**Prep time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook time:** 50 minutes  
**Calories:** 521 per serving  
**Macros per serving:** 49g protein · 54g carbs · 10g fat  
**Serves:** 4

---

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Pasta (dry weight) | 200g |
| Chicken breast, diced | 600g |
| Mozzarella, grated | 80g |
| Onion, diced | 1 |
| Peppers, diced | 2 |
| Lemon & herb seasoning packet | 1 |
| Tinned chopped tomatoes | 400g |
| Lemon & herb sauce | 125g |
| Salt and black pepper | to taste |

---

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 190°C / Fan 170°C.

2. **Recommended:** Par-cook the pasta in boiling salted water until just under al dente — about 2 minutes less than the packet says. Drain well.

3. Add the pasta, chicken, onion, peppers, seasoning packet, tinned tomatoes and lemon & herb sauce to a large ovenproof dish. Season with salt and pepper and mix well.

4. Top with the grated mozzarella. Cover tightly with foil.

5. Bake for 50 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is bubbling.

---

## Notes

- Pre-cooking the pasta makes a real difference — straight-in pasta goes soft and stodgy.
- Schwartz do a lemon & herb seasoning packet; Loyd Grossman or Sacla do a decent lemon & herb pasta sauce.
- Works well with penne or rigatoni — shapes that hold the sauce.
