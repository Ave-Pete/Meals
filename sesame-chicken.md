# Sesame Chicken

Crispy chicken pieces coated in a thick, sweet, sticky sesame sauce. Proper takeaway vibes made at home. Banging.

**Source:** Louis Howard PT (credit: @theconwaycuisine)  
**Tags:** Chicken, Fakeaway, High Protein, Chinese  
**Equipment:** Hob · Air fryer option below

**Prep time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook time:** 20 minutes  
**Calories:** ~800 per serving (half recipe) / ~500–550 with lighter swaps  
**Macros (half recipe):** 58g protein · 83g carbs · 26g fat  
**Serves:** 2

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Chicken thighs, chopped into bite-sized pieces | 500g |
| Eggs | 2 |
| Cornflour | 60g |
| Salt | large pinch |

### Sauce

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Ketchup | 60ml |
| Soy sauce | 60ml |
| Honey | 60ml |
| Brown sugar | 12g |
| Rice vinegar | 10ml |
| Garlic cloves, minced | 3 |
| Sesame oil | 5ml |
| Cornflour (to thicken) | 1 tbsp |
| Sesame seeds | pinch |

---

## Method

1. Cut the chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces. Add to a bowl with the eggs, cornflour and salt. Mix until well coated.

2. Fry the chicken pieces in a hot pan until crispy and golden all over. Cook in batches — don't crowd the pan.

3. In a separate pan, combine the ketchup, soy sauce, honey, brown sugar, rice vinegar, garlic and sesame oil. Bring to a gentle simmer.

4. Mix the cornflour with a splash of cold water to make a smooth paste and stir into the sauce. Simmer until thickened.

5. Add the crispy chicken to the sauce and toss until everything is well coated.

6. Finish with a pinch of sesame seeds and serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- A splash of soy sauce and a squeeze of lemon juice stirred through at the end takes it up another level.
- The calories look high mainly because of the honey, cornflour and chicken thighs — easy to lighten up if needed (see below).

## Lighter Swaps

- Swap chicken thighs for chicken breast
- Reduce honey to 30–40ml
- Use less cornflour in the coating
- Estimated with swaps: ~500–550 kcal per serving

## Air Fryer

Coat the chicken as normal. Preheat to 200°C. Spray the basket lightly with oil and add the chicken pieces in a single layer. Cook for 12–15 minutes, shaking halfway, until crispy and golden. Make the sauce on the hob as normal, then toss the air-fried chicken through it. Gets just as crispy with far less oil.
