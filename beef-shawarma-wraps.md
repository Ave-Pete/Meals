# Beef Shawarma Wraps

Absolutely banging. Proper fakeaway vibes but doesn't ruin your calories. Easy 9/10.

**Source:** [Louis Howard PT](https://www.youtube.com/@LouisHowardpt) (credit: @derevensky.food)  
**Tags:** Beef, Fakeaway, High Protein, Middle Eastern  
**Equipment:** Oven, hob

**Prep time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook time:** 40 minutes  
**Calories:** ~650 per wrap  
**Macros per wrap:** 48g protein · 45g carbs · 27g fat  
**Serves:** 2

---

## Ingredients

### Wraps

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Lean beef mince (5%) | 400g |
| Flatbreads | 2 |
| Tomatoes, diced | 250g |
| Red onion, sliced | ½–1 |

### Spices

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Paprika | 1 tsp |
| Chilli powder | 1 tsp |
| Black pepper | 1 tsp |
| Dried garlic | 1 tsp |
| Salt | to taste |

### Tzatziki

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Greek yogurt | 200g |
| Cucumber, grated | 3 tbsp |
| Garlic bulbs, roasted | 2 whole |
| Dried mint | 1 tsp |
| Dill | 1 tbsp |
| Olive oil | 2 tbsp |
| Lemon juice | 1 tbsp |
| Salt and pepper | to taste |

---

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C / Fan 180°C. Cook the beef with all the spices, tomatoes and garlic cloves for 40 minutes.

2. Mix all the tzatziki ingredients together — yogurt, grated cucumber, roasted garlic, herbs, lemon juice and olive oil. Season and set aside.

3. Warm the flatbreads in the oven for a couple of minutes.

4. Load up: tzatziki first, then beef, then sliced red onion and tomatoes. Wrap, eat, probably make another.

---

## Notes

- Roast the garlic bulbs whole — squeeze the soft cloves straight into the tzatziki.
- Grate the cucumber and squeeze out excess water before mixing into the tzatziki or it'll go watery.

## Lower Calorie Swaps

- Use a low-cal wrap instead of flatbread
- 0% Greek yogurt in the tzatziki
- Reduce or skip the olive oil
- Swap beef mince for chicken
