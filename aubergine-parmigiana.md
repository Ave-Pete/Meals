# Aubergine Parmigiana

A lighter take on this Italian classic — baking the aubergine instead of frying keeps all the comfort with far fewer calories.

**Source:** Hairy Dieters: Simple Healthy Food  
**Tags:** Vegetarian, Italian, Mediterranean, Summer, Winter

**Prep time:** 20 minutes  
**Cook time:** 1 hour 15 minutes  
**Calories:** 325 per serving  
**Equipment:** Oven, hob · Air fryer option for aubergine slices below

---

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | 2 servings | 4 servings (base) | 6 servings | 8 servings |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Olive oil (for aubergines) | ½ tbsp | 1 tbsp | 1½ tbsp | 2 tbsp |
| Aubergines, sliced into ½–1cm rounds | 1½ | 3 | 4½ | 6 |
| Fresh rosemary & oregano sprigs | a few | a few | several | several |
| Sea salt and black pepper | to taste | to taste | to taste | to taste |

### Sauce

| Ingredient | 2 servings | 4 servings (base) | 6 servings | 8 servings |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Olive oil | ½ tsp | 1 tsp | 1½ tsp | 2 tsp |
| Onion, finely chopped | ½ | 1 | 1½ | 2 |
| Garlic cloves, finely chopped | 1½ | 3 | 4½ | 6 |
| Dried oregano | ½ tsp | 1 tsp | 1½ tsp | 2 tsp |
| Cinnamon | small pinch | generous pinch | ½ tsp | ½ tsp |
| Tinned tomatoes | 200g | 400g | 600g | 800g |
| Red wine | 50ml | 100ml | 150ml | 200ml |

### To Assemble

| Ingredient | 2 servings | 4 servings (base) | 6 servings | 8 servings |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fresh basil leaves | small bunch | small bunch | large bunch | large bunch |
| Mozzarella balls, drained & torn | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |

---

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C / Fan 180°C / Gas 6. Mix the olive oil with a tablespoon of water. Lay the aubergine slices on 2–3 baking trays lined with baking parchment. Brush sparingly with the oil and water mix, season with salt and pepper, and scatter over the broken-up herb sprigs. Bake for 25–30 minutes until squidgy in the middle and nicely browned. Discard the herbs.

2. Meanwhile, make the sauce. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan, add the onion with a splash of water and sauté gently until translucent. Add the garlic and sauté for a further 2–3 minutes. Sprinkle in the oregano and cinnamon, then pour in the tomatoes and red wine. Rinse the tin with 100ml of water and add to the sauce. Season, bring to the boil, cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove the lid and continue simmering until well reduced.

3. To assemble, spread a spoonful of sauce over the base of an ovenproof dish. Add a layer of aubergines and a couple of torn basil leaves. Continue layering, pressing down well between each layer, until all the aubergines and sauce are used up.

4. Top with more basil and dot with the torn mozzarella. Bake for 25–30 minutes until golden and bubbling.

---

## Notes

- Mixing the oil with water before brushing the aubergines is the key trick for keeping the calorie count down — aubergines are notorious for absorbing oil.
- Press down firmly between layers when assembling to get a good, compact result.

## Air Fryer

The aubergine slices can be done in the air fryer instead of the oven, which is faster and frees up the oven for the final bake. Preheat to 200°C. Brush the slices with the oil-water mix, season, and cook in batches for 15–18 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and tender. Then assemble and finish the bake in the oven as normal. With a dual-basket air fryer you can run two batches at once and cut the aubergine prep time significantly.
