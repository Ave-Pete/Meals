# Spanish-Style Baked Chicken Thighs

A simple one-pan Spanish-inspired bake — chicken thighs, potatoes and peppers all roasted together with garlic, paprika and lemon. Easy to serve, easy to clean up.

**Source:** Vikalinka  
**Tags:** Main, Spanish, Mediterranean, One Pan

**Prep time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook time:** 50–60 minutes  
**Serves:** 6  
**Equipment:** Oven, roasting tin

---

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Garlic cloves, minced | 3 |
| Lemon (juice and zest) | 1 |
| Dried oregano | 1 tbsp |
| Fresh oregano sprigs (optional) | 2 |
| Fresh thyme sprigs (optional) | 2–3 |
| Spanish paprika (dulce/sweet smoked) | 2 tsp |
| Olive oil | 3 tbsp |
| Chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on | 1kg (about 6) |
| Red and yellow peppers, sliced | 4 |
| Red onion, cut into thick wedges | 1 |
| Baby potatoes, halved if large | 500g |
| Salt | to taste |

---

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C / Fan 180°C / Gas 6.

2. Mix the lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, dried oregano, paprika and olive oil together in a small bowl.

3. Arrange the chicken thighs, potatoes, onion and peppers in a large roasting tin. Drizzle or brush over the herb mixture and toss everything to coat evenly. Season well with salt. Tuck in any fresh herb sprigs and lemon wedges if using.

4. Roast for 50–60 minutes until the chicken juices run clear when pierced at the thickest point and the potatoes are tender.

5. If you want crispier skin, pop under the grill for a further 2–3 minutes at the end.

---

## Notes

- Bone-in, skin-on thighs give the best results — the fat bastes everything as it cooks.
- If using boneless thighs, reduce the temperature to 180°C and par-boil the potatoes for 10 minutes first.
- Leftovers keep in the fridge for up to 4 days or can be frozen for 3 months.
