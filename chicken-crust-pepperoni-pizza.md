# High Protein Chicken Crust Pepperoni Pizza

A protein monster. Chicken mince forms the crust — crispy edges, proper pizza vibes, and 44g of protein per quarter.

**Source:** [Louis Howard PT](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/jFvVwhsyiyQ)  
**Tags:** High Protein, Pizza, Fakeaway, Main, Low Carb  
**Equipment:** Oven, grill (optional)

**Prep time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook time:** 35 minutes  
**Calories:** 438 per serving (¼ pizza)  
**Macros per serving:** 44g protein · 9g carbs · 26g fat  
**Serves:** 4

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken Crust

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Chicken mince | 450g |
| Eggs | 2 large |
| Parmesan, grated | 50g |
| Garlic powder | 1 tsp |
| Italian seasoning | 1 tsp |
| Salt | ½ tsp |
| Black pepper | ½ tsp |

### Toppings

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Pizza sauce | 120g |
| Mozzarella, grated | 100g |
| Turkey pepperoni slices | 20 |

---

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 200°C / Fan 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking parchment.

2. Mix the chicken mince, eggs, parmesan, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper together until well combined.

3. Press the mixture onto the tray into a thin circle or rectangle — the thinner it is, the crispier it gets.

4. Bake for 25 minutes until golden. Remove and leave to rest for 5 minutes.

5. Increase the oven to 230°C. Spread the pizza sauce over the crust, scatter over the mozzarella and lay on the pepperoni.

6. Bake for a further 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbling. Optional: finish under the grill for 1–2 minutes for extra crispness.

7. Cool slightly, divide into 4 and serve.

---

## Notes

- Thin = crispy. Don't leave the crust too thick or the middle stays soft and wet.
- Turkey pepperoni keeps the calories lower than regular pepperoni — regular works fine if you're not counting.
- Chicken mince can be made by blitzing chicken breasts in a food processor.

## Air Fryer

Press the chicken mixture into a round that fits your air fryer basket. Cook at 200°C for 18–20 minutes until golden and cooked through. Add the toppings and cook for a further 5 minutes until the cheese is melted. Much faster than the oven and gets a better crust on the base.
