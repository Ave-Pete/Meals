# Tortilla Pizza Pie

A quick high-protein pizza bake using tortilla wraps as the base. Louis's altered version of an older recipe — calorie-dense per whole pie, so cut into slices.

**Source:** [Louis Howard PT](https://www.youtube.com/@LouisHowardpt)  
**Tags:** High Protein, Pizza, Fakeaway, Main  
**Equipment:** Oven, grill

**Prep time:** 5 minutes  
**Cook time:** 25–30 minutes  
**Calories:** 310 per slice (4 slices)  
**Macros per slice:** 20g protein · 18g carbs · 16g fat  
**Serves:** 2–4

---

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Tortilla wraps | 2 |
| Eggs | 2–3 |
| Ham | 100g |
| Mozzarella | 100g |
| Pepperoni | 50g |
| Pizza sauce | 25–50g |

---

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C / Fan 180°C.

2. Mix the eggs, ham, mozzarella, pepperoni and pizza sauce together in a bowl.

3. Lay the tortilla wraps in an ovenproof dish or on a lined baking tray. Spread the mixture over the top.

4. Bake for 20–25 minutes until set and golden at the edges.

5. Finish under the grill for a further 5 minutes until the top is bubbling and slightly charred.

6. Cut into 4 slices and serve.

---

## Notes

- Don't eat the whole thing in one go — it's calorie-dense as a full pie.
- Pizza sauce quantity to taste — 25g keeps it lighter, 50g if you want more flavour.
- Works well with any cured meat: swap ham or pepperoni for chorizo or salami.

## Air Fryer

Preheat to 190°C. Place one tortilla in the basket, spread the mixture on top. Cook for 12–15 minutes until set and golden. No need for the grill finish — the air fryer browns the top naturally. Do one wrap at a time given basket size.
